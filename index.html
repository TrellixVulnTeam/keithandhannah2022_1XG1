About Our Wedding

We are so excited to have everyone we love together in one place. Chicago is a city we love, and is where we have lived for our entire relationship. We're tying the knot at The Bottom Lounge, a music venue where we've been able to indulge our passion for live music many times over the years. The Bottom Lounge is located in Chicago's West Loop, a popular place for sight seeing, going out, and some of the city's very best places to eat.

We will share more details on the timeline, weekend events, and ceremony specifics as the date approaches. For guests travelling from out of town, we will also provide more details on lodging, activities, suggestions, and logistics as the date gets closer.

About Us

How We Met

We matched on a dating app, OKCupid, in May 2015. Keith had been celebrating Mother's Day with his mom, so was feeling enough liquid courage to suggest we meet up for a drink. Hannah was a little hesitant, as it was her first time using a dating app, but after some encouragement from her roommate, she agreed. We met up at the Burwood Tap in Lincoln Park, and talked about beer, biking, music, and more. We began "officially" dating a few months later, and the rest is history :)

The Proposal

Keith planned to propose during a trip to Europe in May 2020, and had the ring and plan all ready to go. However, like most things in 2020, COVID-19 cancelled the trip and that plan. Since our big European trip was cancelled, we decided to take a socially-distant long weekend at a lakehouse near Lake Geneva, Wisconsin. Keith proposed on July 25 by the campfire.
